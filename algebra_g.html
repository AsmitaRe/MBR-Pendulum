Algebraic Stage : Equation of motion

The motion of the bob is determined by the torque due to the forces acting on it. The general equation of motion of the pendulum is,
$\tau = I\alpha = mL^{2}\frac{d^{2}\theta}{dt^{2}} \hskip 15em$ ..eq(1)
In the presence of gravitational forces, $ F_{g} = - mg $

The cosine component of the gravitational force is balanced by the tension in the string. While the sine component provides the necessary restoring force that sets the pendulum in motion.
Net Torque, $\hskip 1 em \tau = -mg_{e}Lsin\theta \hskip 10em$ ..eq(2)

Equation of motion of the pendulum after insertaing the details of the system is,
$$ mL\frac{d^{2}\theta }{dt^{2}} + mg_{e}sin\theta = 0$$
